Site Plan: Basic Soccer

Site Name

Basic Soccer

I chose this name because it is simple and clearly states what the site is about. The word "basic" indicates that it is for people who are just starting to learn about soccer.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to teach the fundamentals of soccer to people who don't know much about the sport. The site will include information on what soccer is, the main rules, player positions, and what equipment is needed to play.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

What are the basic rules I need to know to understand a soccer match?

Scenario 2

Which position should I play if I am a beginner?

Scenario 3

What things do I need to buy to start playing soccer?
